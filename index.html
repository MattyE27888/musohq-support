Common questions

I bought MusoHQ Pro but it's not showing on a new device.
Open MusoHQ, go to the Upgrade screen in Settings, and tap Restore Purchases. This restores any past purchase tied to your Apple ID.

Where does my invoice, band, and expense data live?
Entirely on your device. MusoHQ has no account and no server — nothing is uploaded anywhere unless you explicitly share it (for example, sending an invoice or exporting a CSV). See the Privacy Policy for full detail.

How does the GST calculation work?
GST is calculated automatically as one-eleventh of the total on any invoice or expense you mark as GST-inclusive. MusoHQ doesn't lodge anything with the ATO on your behalf — it's a record-keeping tool, not a tax agent.

Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled entirely by Apple, not the developer. Request one at reportaproblem.apple.com.
