Last updated: 7 July 2026

These terms cover your use of MusoHQ, an invoicing, band payout, and tax record-keeping app for working musicians.

What MusoHQ is for

MusoHQ helps you log gigs, raise invoices, split band payouts, track expenses, and estimate GST and super guarantee on your income. It's a record-keeping tool, not a tax agent, an accountant, or a legal service — you're responsible for checking figures before you lodge anything with the ATO or rely on them for a real transaction.

Your data

MusoHQ stores everything locally on your device. There's no account and no server, so you're responsible for backing up your own data (MusoHQ includes a manual backup/restore feature for this). If you delete the app or lose your device without a backup, your data is gone — the developer can't recover it.

MusoHQ is provided "as is." While it's built carefully and tested before release, the developer doesn't guarantee it's free of bugs or that any calculation (GST, super, totals) is error-free. Always sanity-check figures that matter, especially at tax time.

Acceptable use

Use MusoHQ for your own gigs, invoicing, and record-keeping. Don't reverse-engineer, resell, or use the app to build a competing product.
